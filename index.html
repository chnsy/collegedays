About

The 36th Foundation Week of the Asian College of Technology (ACT) typically celebrates the institution's commitment to education, innovation, and community. During this weeks, various activities and events are organized to engage students, faculty, and alumni, showcasing the college's achievements and fostering school spirit.

Cultural Performances: Showcasing the diverse talents of students through dance, music, and theater.

Sports Competitions: Encouraging teamwork and healthy competition among different departments.

Exhibitions and Workshops: Highlighting student projects and offering skill-building opportunities.

Guest Speakers and Seminars: Featuring industry professionals who share insights and experiences relevant to students' fields of study.

This serves as a platform for building camaraderie and celebrating the vibrant culture of ACT, while also promoting academic excellence and community involvement.
